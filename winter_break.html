India

Part One

I spent the first two and half weeks of my trip in India volunteering, traveling, and learning in the city of Mumbai. My group of 12 and I traveled to Elephanta Island, the Sassoon Docks, ate at local restaurants, visited local street markets, and visited different slums throughout the city. We prepared a variety of lesson plans and began teaching 1st-8th graders in the Kalwa slums and Ashte village.

Part Two
