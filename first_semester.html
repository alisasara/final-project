First Semester

During the first semester of my gap year, I lived in a hostel in the center of Jerusalem. I took classes at Hebrew University in Jerusalem, including Hebrew language courses, History courses, and biblical studies. While living in Jerusalem, I learned to navigate the Israeli bus system, build upon my Hebrew language skills, learn to manage a budget in a foreign country, and develop the confidence to travel and venture around a fairly new country. I had the opportunity to immerse myself in Israeli culture, celebrate the Jewish holidays in a city that observes the holidays, travel around the country with my friends, and create life lasting memories.
